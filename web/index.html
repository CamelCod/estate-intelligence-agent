FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to replace my cameras?
No. Estate Intelligence works with any IP camera that supports RTSP — which includes most Hikvision, Dahua, Reolink, Uniview, and Axis cameras. If your NVR supports RTSP streams, you're compatible.
Is my footage stored anywhere?
No. We extract single frames for event analysis and store only the text description of what happened. Your video footage stays on your NVR at your property. We never store, copy, or transmit video.
What if something serious happens — a break-in or emergency?
We escalate immediately. If a camera detects a genuine intrusion or physical emergency, you receive an instant WhatsApp message — not just an evening briefing. You remain in control for any irreversible action.
Can I ask questions in Arabic?
Yes. Standard and Estate plans include Arabic briefings and the Q&A interface accepts Arabic text. Briefings are delivered in your preferred language.
How does the free trial work?
You get 14 days of full service at no cost. No credit card is required to start. On day 12, we'll remind you to add a payment method if you'd like to continue. Cancel any time — no questions asked.
Is this a SIRA-licensed security system?
No — and intentionally so. Estate Intelligence is a property awareness and staff accountability service. We provide daily briefings about home activity, not a licensed SIRA security monitoring service. This distinction matters legally in the UAE.
