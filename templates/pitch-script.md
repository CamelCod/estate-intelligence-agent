# Pitch Script

## WhatsApp Warm Outreach (first message — 3 sentences max)

"Hey [NAME], I'm building something for villa owners in Dubai and thought of you.
It's a daily WhatsApp briefing that tells you what happened at home — staff arrivals,
deliveries, anything unusual — all from your existing cameras. Would a quick look be useful?"

---

## Discovery Call Script (20 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for making time. Quick context: I'm not selling a camera system —
you keep everything you have. I just want to understand your situation and see
if what I've built would actually be useful for you."

### Discovery Questions (8 min)
1. "How often are you away from the villa — a few days a month, or more?"
2. "Do you have cameras at home already? Do you actually use the app?"
3. "When you're away, what's the thing you most wish you knew about what's happening at home?"
4. "Do you have staff — a cleaner, a nanny, a driver? Do you ever wonder if they showed up?"

### Pivot to Product (5 min)
"What I've built is basically a daily briefing that tells you all of that — automatically.
Every evening, you get a WhatsApp message: who arrived, who left, if anything unusual happened.
And you can reply and ask questions like 'did my delivery come?' or 'was anyone in the garage today?'
It works on your existing cameras. I don't touch the footage."

### Objection Handling
- "How is this different from my camera app?" →
  "Your camera app shows you footage. This tells you what happened. Big difference when you're in a meeting in London."
- "What if I'm worried about something serious?" →
  "We flag it immediately and you decide what to do. This isn't a replacement for real response — it's the awareness layer before you need it."

### Close (5 min)
"I'm running a beta with a few villa owners in Dubai right now at zero cost for the first month.
If it's useful, it's AED [TIER_PRICE]/month after that — less than one session with a cleaner.
Want me to set it up for your property this week?"

---

[ATTACH SAMPLE BRIEFING]
