# Daily Briefing Template

## Format Rules
- Max 250 words
- No markdown (no **, no -, no #)
- Short paragraphs, one idea per paragraph
- Max 3 emoji per message
- Always ends with a question to encourage engagement
- Tone: calm, professional, like a trusted house manager

---

## Template

Good evening, [OWNER_FIRST_NAME]. Here's your home briefing for [DAY, DATE].

[STAFF_SUMMARY]
Examples:
- "Mariam arrived at 8:12am and left at 4:55pm — all as expected."
- "The AC technician arrived at 10:30am, was on-site for 1h 20min, and left. No other visitors."
- "Noura did not arrive today. No activity was recorded at the front entrance during her expected window (7am–9am)."

[PACKAGE_DELIVERIES]
Examples:
- "A delivery was made at 2:17pm and collected by Mariam 8 minutes later."
- "No deliveries recorded today."
- "⚠️ A package was left at the door at 3:40pm and has not been moved."

[ANOMALIES — only include if present]
Examples:
- "One camera (side gate) was offline between 1:10pm and 1:48pm."
- "Motion was detected in the garage at 11:30pm. No person visible in the following frames."

[PROACTIVE_TIP]
Example: "Tip: You can ask me anything — try 'Did anyone go into the garden today?'"

Is there anything specific you'd like me to check from today? 🏡
