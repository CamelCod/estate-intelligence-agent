# Sales Kit — Estate Intelligence Agent

## Positioning

**Core statement:** "Estate intelligence for UAE villa owners who can't always be home."

**What it is:** A daily WhatsApp briefing + conversational Q&A that turns existing cameras into a smart home monitoring service. No new hardware. No subscriptions to multiple apps. Just one message every evening telling you what happened.

**What it is NOT:** A security service, a camera system, a surveillance tool, a smart home hub.

---

## Target Customer Profile

**Primary:** UAE villa or compound owner
- Travels frequently (1+ week per month)
- Employs domestic staff (cleaner, driver, nanny, gardener)
- Has existing CCTV cameras (any brand — Hikvision, Dahua, Axis, etc.)
- Wants accountability without confrontation
- Frustrated by camera apps they never actually check

**Secondary:** Property managers overseeing multiple villas or compounds.

---

## Primary Pains (sell to these)

1. **"I don't know if staff actually showed up when I'm away"**
   — Staff accountability is the #1 conversation starter

2. **"I have cameras but I never actually watch them"**
   — Raw footage is not the same as awareness

3. **"I worry about deliveries, visitors, things happening at home"**
   — Uncertainty while traveling is the core emotional trigger

4. **"I don't trust my cameras to alert me to things that matter"**
   — Camera push notifications are noisy and useless

## Sample Briefing (attach to all outreach)

```
Good evening, Ahmed. Here's your home briefing for Thursday, 3 April.

Khalid arrived at 7:02am and left at 7:08pm — a full day, as expected. Today is not Mariam's scheduled day.

An Amazon delivery arrived at 7:58am and was brought inside by Khalid at 9:44am. No other deliveries or visitors today.

One thing to note: the side gate camera went offline at 11:30am and came back online at 1:21pm. The gap was about 1 hour 50 minutes. This may be a connectivity issue worth checking.

Tip: You can ask me anything from today — for example, "What time did Khalid leave?" or "Was anyone in the garden today?"

Anything specific you'd like to know about today? 🏡
```

## Discovery Questions (warm call)

1. "How often are you away from the villa — a few days a month, or more?"
2. "Do you have cameras at home already? Do you actually use the app?"
3. "When you're away, what's the thing you most wish you knew about what's happening at home?"
4. "Do you have staff — a cleaner, a nanny, a driver? Do you ever wonder if they showed up?"

---

## Objection Handling

**"I already have cameras."**
→ "Great. We make them actually useful. When did you last open your camera app?"

**"Is this expensive?"**
→ "Less than one hour of a cleaner's time per month. Starter is AED 199/month for up to 3 cameras."

**"Is my data safe?"**
→ "We work on event summaries only, not video storage. Your footage stays on your NVR — we never touch it."

**"What if something serious happens?"**
→ "We flag it immediately and you decide what to do. This is the awareness layer before you need to act."

**"How is this different from my camera app?"**
→ "Your camera app shows you footage. This tells you what happened. Big difference when you're in a meeting in London."

## Sales Disqualifiers (stop immediately)

- Commercial workplace surveillance → refer elsewhere
- Audio recording questions → decline, legal exposure
- Real-time intrusion alerts → not our product, refer to security company
- No existing cameras and no intention to install → Not Yet lead
