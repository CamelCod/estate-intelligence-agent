# Positioning Rules

## Core Positioning Statement
"Estate intelligence for UAE villa owners who can't always be home."

## Target Customer
UAE villa or compound owner who:
- Travels frequently (1+ week per month)
- Employs domestic staff (cleaner, driver, nanny, gardener)
- Has existing CCTV cameras (any brand)
- Wants accountability without confrontation

## Primary Pains (ranked)
1. "I don't know if staff actually showed up when I'm away"
2. "I have cameras but I never actually watch them"
3. "I worry about deliveries, visitors, things happening at home"
4. "I don't trust my cameras to alert me to things that matter"

## Brand Voice
- Calm and professional (like a trusted estate manager, not a startup)
- Specific and concrete (never vague promises — always a real example)
- Understated — never "revolutionary" or "AI-powered" in first contact
- Arabic-aware — be ready to switch to Arabic for certain segments

## Approved Phrases
- "estate intelligence"
- "home briefing"
- "staff accountability"
- "peace of mind layer"
- "property awareness"
- "conversational home overview"
- "know what happened while you were away"
- "your home, explained daily"

## Forbidden Phrases (customer-facing)
- "security" (SIRA risk)
- "surveillance"
- "monitoring system"
- "tracking"
- "spy"
- "CCTV agent" (internal term only)
- "AI-powered security"
- "smart camera system" (too generic)

## Positioning vs Competitors
| Them | Us |
|---|---|
| Camera apps (Hikvision, Dahua) | Raw footage alerts vs conversational briefings |
| Security companies | Guards and alarms vs intelligence and awareness |
| Smart home systems | Complex installs vs works on your existing cameras |
| Nothing (most common) | Flying blind vs knowing every day |

## Objection Handling
- "I already have cameras" → "Great. We make them actually useful — when did you last watch the footage?"
- "Is this expensive?" → "Less than one hour of a cleaner's time per month."
- "Is my data safe?" → "We work on event summaries only, not video storage. Your footage stays on your NVR."
- "What if something serious happens?" → "We escalate immediately and you can always call us."
