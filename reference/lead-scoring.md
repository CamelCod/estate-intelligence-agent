# Lead Scoring Matrix

## Score: Hot (proceed immediately to demo)
All of the following:
- Villa or compound (not apartment)
- Has 2+ existing cameras with accessible RTSP stream
- Travels internationally 1+ week/month
- Employs domestic staff
- Current solution: nothing or default camera app they never use

## Score: Warm (send explainer, follow up in 7 days)
Most of the following:
- Villa or large apartment
- Has cameras (even if RTSP uncertain)
- Travel frequency unknown or occasional
- Has staff or regular visitors
- Curious but no urgent pain yet

## Score: Not Yet (follow up in 30 days)
Any of the following:
- Apartment only with 1 camera
- No existing cameras and unwilling to install any
- No domestic staff and minimal visitor activity
- Currently satisfied with existing solution
- Price-sensitive without strong pain
